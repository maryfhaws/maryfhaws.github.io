Exploring AI Art .

Website: Exploring the World of AI Art Generators

Collaborators: Anjali Rao & Sara Sarmiento

This site was developed as a final project for my IT course during my Masters at Pratt. My collaborators and I are all artist interested in the impacts--both positive and negative--of new technologies like artificial intelligence and art generators on the artists' practices and livelihoods. Our research explores pros and cons, legal considerations, and case studies of writing prompts for particular ai-generated outcomes. We concluded that AI can serve to support and expand the creativity of artists, that it is likely to change the field and how artists work, and that it is imperative that the law is adapted with considerations of every creator involved in the process.
