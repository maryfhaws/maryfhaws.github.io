. Photography .

As an amateur photographer (one who relies mostly on the iPhone lenses and VSCO film filters), my goal is to capture experience in the form of a visual journal. Through this medium, I experiment with composition, colors, and forms, and strive to enhance the essence of the subject. Personal perception and feeling is also injected, impulsively or contemplatively. The value in this visual journal is in solidifying conscious experience and memories, while lingering unconscious processes continue to develop meaning until such experiences are ready to be expressed: my photography is often the spark that informs my art by allowing me to immerse myself in life, to appreciate nature and my small role in its vastness.
