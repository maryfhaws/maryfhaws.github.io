About

Hi! I'm Mary --

— a UX designer and artist. My experience and interest in cognitive science, art, technology, and ethics informs my design practice. Combining these skills allows me to take a systems-thinking approach to design, without missing the finer details. I'm equipped for each step of the process from user research to UI design.

Because I believe in a holistic approach, my portfolio isn't just about my design work. My art and photography are key in shaping my design philosophy, so I invite you to explore these as well.

Please send me an email or LinkedIn message if you want to talk!
