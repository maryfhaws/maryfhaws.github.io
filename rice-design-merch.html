R!CE DESIGN MERCH .

Instagram: @ricedesignmerch

Rice Design launched two collections of merchandise in 2021 under the brand R!CE DESIGN MERCH, and I managed the Instagram campaigns in addition to designing items. Rather than creating merchandise only for our club members, our designs have campus-wide appeal by referencing Rice culture in general. Rice Design has spearheaded this movement to unite Rice students and inspire school pride after over a year of remote learning. Now, more than ever, it is important for our campus to come together and appreciate our school and bonds with each other, which was the motivation behind our collections.
