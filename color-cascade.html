Website: Cascading Colors

This site, constructed for an IT course for my Masters at Pratt, was inspired by my interests in painting, the psychology of perception, and CSS, with which I was just becoming familiar. I wanted to capture the similarities between CSS and painting, particularly between the DOM structure and color derivation to produce visuals. Much like the "cascading" of styles applied to nodes and their children in the DOM, color theory outlines the "cascade" of primary colors as they influence every other color in the visible spectrum. My background in cognitive sciences and human-centered design in general also informed this project.
